Al-Shifa: A Sanctuary for the Heart

“Verily, with hardship comes ease” (Quran 94:6)

Your Path to Healing

O soul in pain, turn to Allah, for He is the Healer of hearts. In your trials, seek His mercy, for “Indeed, Allah is with the patient” (Quran 2:153). Let your heart find solace in His remembrance, for in His truth lies your salvation. You are not alone; this sanctuary is a place of hope, patience, and light.
