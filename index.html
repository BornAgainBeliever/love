Al-Noor: Sanctuary of the Heart

“Indeed, in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest” (Quran 13:28)

A Haven for Your Soul

O heart weighed by sorrow, find refuge in Allah’s boundless mercy. “When My servants ask you about Me, indeed I am near” (Quran 2:186). Your pain is known, your tears are seen, and your soul is cherished. In this sanctuary, let the light of faith guide you to peace, patience, and salvation. Trust in Allah, for He is Al-Shafi, the Healer of all wounds.
